Around the world in 42 chips

Welcome to AROUND THE WORLD IN 42 CHIPS!

Discussing with your friend whether to travel by train or by plane? Why not decide it on a super fun game of CONNECT4?

Each player clicks on a column to add its chip. The first player to stack four consecutive chips on a column, on a row or on a diagonal WINS!

BONUS

On each round, a new T-Shirt will be added to your luggage. This way you are ready to go! Also check your 'Travel log'. It grows as you travel to different destinations in each round!

Your passport is ready! Get your luggage and your camera and don't be late!
